Diets

The Ketogenic Diet

A very low carbohydrate and high protein and fat diet is known as the ketogenic diet.
The goal is to trick the body into going into a state of ketosis, when it uses fat for fuel instead of the body's typical supply of glucose and other carbs high in glucose.

Read more

Vegan

Veganism is a diet and way of life which forbids the use of any animal products, including dairy, honey, and eggs.
When practiced correctly, the vegan diet can be sustaining, filling, and have a variety of health benefits, such as reducing body weight, blood pressure, and the prevalence of cardiovascular disease.

Read more

Whole Food

The most fundamental and straightforward eating style is the whole food diet. It entails consuming food in its natural state with little processing.
As processed foods, sweets, fast foods, and fried foods are removed from your plate and you return to simple, clean eating, a whole food diet tends to improve your health and vitality.

Supplements

L-Carnitine

L-Carnitine is a food supplement in liquid form recommended for athletes and active people, for more energy and fat burning.
It is recommended for burning fat together with physical exercises and a proper diet, but also in moments of fatigue and mental exhaustion.

Creatine Monohydrate

Creatine is a food supplement that contains 100% pure micronized creatine monohydrate, without sugar, fats or additives.
Increases the intensity of training and Stimulates growth in muscle mass. Administer 1 scoop (5 g) dissolved in 300 ml of water or juice.

Pre-Workout

This pre-workout has a complex formula, containing effective and powerful ingredients to support an intense and long-lasting workout.
Explosive energy during training, Stimulates focus and concentration and provides a strong premix of vitamins and minerals.

Hydrowhey Protein

Hydrowhey Protein Premium is a dietary supplement based on hydrolyzed whey protein powder.
Contains 33 g of WPH hydrolyzed whey protein in each 35 g serving, Low content of Carbohydrates and Fats. It is widely used in diets to replace a meal and to maintain quality muscle mass.

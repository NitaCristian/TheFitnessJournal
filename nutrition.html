Diets

The Ketogenic Diet

A very low carbohydrate and high protein and fat diet is known as the ketogenic diet. The goal is to trick the body into going into a state of ketosis, when it uses fat for fuel instead of the body's typical supply of glucose and other carbs high in glucose.

Vegan

Veganism is a diet and way of life which forbids the use of any animal products, including dairy, honey, and eggs. When practiced correctly, the vegan diet can be sustaining, filling, and have a variety of health benefits, such as reducing body weight, blood pressure, and the prevalence of cardiovascular disease.

Read more

Whole Food

The most fundamental and straightforward eating style is the whole food diet. It entails consuming food in its natural state with little processing. As processed foods, sweets, fast foods, and fried foods are removed from your plate and you return to simple, clean eating, a whole food diet tends to improve your health and vitality.

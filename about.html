About Us

The Fitness Report is a small online publication started by a group of passionate people. Our goal is to deliver quality and science-based information to a broad audience in an effort to change minds and empower people to lead better, healthier, and more satisfying lives.

It all started with when people at our local gym kept asking us for advice on exercises and nutrition. We then decided to spread our knowledge with a broader audience, but also expand it with the help of people around the world.

Our readers may come from different backgrounds with different opportunities, but we are sure that we have everything for everybody.

FAQ

Is the provided content meant to serve as medical advice? Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional prior to beginning any diet or exercise program or taking any dietary supplement. The content on our website is for informational and educational purposes only and is not intended as medical advice or to replace a relationship with a qualified healthcare professional.
Can I unsubscribe from the newsletter? No.
Is the information updated regularly? Board-certified physicians and nutrition and exercise healthcare specialists analyze Fitness Report articles. The information is extensive and accurate, representing the most recent evidence-based research, according to medical experts. Before being published and after significant updates, content is verified.
